# Developer Intake and Routing

## Purpose

Standardize how new developers and projects are onboarded into the platform — ensuring every engagement starts with a complete intake, clear routing to functional teams, and defined response SLAs.

## Scope

All new developer engagements across all entities. Initiated by the Development Concierge upon first contact or project kickoff.

## Procedure

1. **Receive inquiry or project kickoff.** Concierge receives a new developer inquiry or an internal project initiation from SHB Group.
2. **Complete developer intake checklist.** Gather all required information per the checklist below.
3. **Classify project type.** Determine which functional teams are needed based on project scope:
   - **Design** — SHB Studio for architectural, interior, or brand design.
   - **Construction** — SHB Inc. (GC/CM) for pre-construction or active construction.
   - **Procurement** — Builiq Inc. for materials, equipment, or vendor sourcing.
   - **Fund** — Fund Management for capital structure, investor relations, or draw schedules.
   - **Showroom** — Sales/Showroom for leasing, display, or event coordination.
4. **Route to functional teams.** Send the intake package to each relevant team with a response deadline per the SLA table.
5. **Confirm receipt.** Each functional team confirms receipt within the routing SLA.
6. **Schedule kickoff.** Concierge schedules a project kickoff meeting with all routed teams within 5 business days of intake completion.
7. **Open project record.** Concierge creates entries in the decision log and any relevant tracking systems (RFI log, CO log, submittal register as applicable).

## Escalation

| Condition | Action |
|---|---|
| Functional team does not confirm receipt within SLA | Concierge escalates to SHB Group (Owner's Rep) |
| Kickoff not scheduled within 5 business days | Concierge escalates to SHB Group (Owner's Rep) |
| Intake incomplete after 3 business days | Concierge sends written reminder to developer; notifies Owner's Rep |
