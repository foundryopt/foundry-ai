# Project Continuity Handoff

## Purpose

Ensure every project transitions cleanly between lifecycle phases — from construction through showroom activation through property management — with no orphaned assets, no lost documentation, and no gaps in ownership or accountability.

## Scope

All projects managed through the Development Concierge. Covers three primary handoff points:
1. Construction → Showroom / Sales
2. Showroom / Sales → Property Management
3. Construction → Property Management (direct, for projects without a showroom phase)

## Control: No Orphaned Asset

**No project may be marked as "handed off" until the receiving team has confirmed acceptance of all documentation, open items, and system access.**

- A project without a confirmed receiving owner is flagged as "orphan risk" in the decision log.
- The Concierge maintains ownership of the handoff until the receiving team signs the acceptance checklist.
- Any open items not resolved at handoff are transferred with written assignment and SLAs.

## Handoff 1: Construction → Showroom / Sales

Triggered when construction reaches substantial completion or a defined pre-leasing milestone.

### Handoff Checklist

| # | Item | Source | Receiving Team |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Certificate of substantial completion | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom |
| 2 | Punch list with status and completion dates | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom |
| 3 | As-built drawings (current set) | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) + SHB Studio | Sales/Showroom |
| 4 | Material and finish schedule (for showroom displays) | SHB Studio (Design) | Sales/Showroom |
| 5 | Approved submittal log (closed items) | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom |
| 6 | Open RFI summary (if any remain) | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom |
| 7 | Open CO summary (if any remain) | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom |
| 8 | Warranty documentation and start dates | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom + Property Management |
| 9 | Key contacts (subs, vendors, consultants) | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom |
| 10 | Access credentials and security protocols | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | Sales/Showroom |

## Handoff 2: Showroom / Sales → Property Management

Triggered when leasing reaches a defined threshold or the showroom phase concludes.

### Handoff Checklist

| # | Item | Source | Receiving Team |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Executed leases and tenant contact list | Sales/Showroom | Property Management |
| 2 | Unit condition reports (photos + notes) | Sales/Showroom | Property Management |
| 3 | Buyer/tenant feedback log | Sales/Showroom | Property Management + Development |
| 4 | Event and vendor participation records | Sales/Showroom | Property Management |
| 5 | Warranty documentation (transferred from Handoff 1) | Sales/Showroom | Property Management |
| 6 | Building systems manuals and O&M documentation | Construction (via Handoff 1) | Property Management |
| 7 | Open items and pending resolutions | Sales/Showroom | Property Management |
| 8 | Access credentials and security protocols | Sales/Showroom | Property Management |

## Handoff 3: Construction → Property Management (Direct)

Triggered at construction close-out for projects without a showroom phase.

### Handoff Checklist

Combines items from Handoff 1 (items 1–10) with Handoff 2 (items 5–8). See also `property-management/ops-property-management-onboarding.md` for the receiving-side checklist.

## Handoff SLAs

| Activity | Timeline | Escalation |
|---|---|---|
| Handoff package assembled by sending team | 10 business days before handoff date | Concierge escalates to Owner's Rep |
| Receiving team reviews package | 5 business days after receipt | Concierge escalates to Owner's Rep |
| Receiving team signs acceptance | 3 business days after review | Concierge escalates to Owner's Rep |
| Open items transferred with SLAs | At acceptance | Concierge monitors until resolved |
| Orphan risk flag if no acceptance | 15 business days after handoff package sent | Concierge escalates to Principal |

## RACI

| Activity | Concierge | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) | SHB Studio (Design) | SHB Group (Owner's Rep) | Sales/Showroom | Property Mgmt |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Initiate handoff process | **R** | I | I | **A** | I | I |
| Assemble handoff package | C | **R** (from construction) | C | I | **R** (from showroom) | — |
| Review handoff package | **R** | — | — | I | **R** (receiving) | **R** (receiving) |
| Sign acceptance checklist | I | — | — | **A** | **R** (receiving) | **R** (receiving) |
| Transfer open items | **R** | **R** (assign) | — | **A** | I | I |
| Monitor open items to close | **R** | I | I | **A** | I | I |
| Flag orphan risk | **R** | — | — | **A** | — | — |

**R** = Responsible, **A** = Accountable, **C** = Consulted, **I** = Informed
