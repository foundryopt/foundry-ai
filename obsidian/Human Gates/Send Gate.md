# Send Gate

**AI** drafts the external message. **Human** sends via GHL or email.

AI cannot send external-facing messages directly — see [[What AI May Not Do]]. Level 4 (GHL Draft-to-Send) is **not active** — see [[Level 4 - GHL Draft-to-Send]].
