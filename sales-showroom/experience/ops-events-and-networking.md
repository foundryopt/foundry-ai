# Events and Networking

## Purpose

Standardize how developer events, investor events, and vendor showcases are planned, executed, and followed up — ensuring consistent brand experience, clear participation rules, and captured business development value.

## Scope

All events hosted at Foundry Rooms locations. Applies to showroom managers, event coordinators, sales teams, and participating vendors.

## Event Types

| Type | Purpose | Typical Audience | Minimum Lead Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Developer Event** | Project unveilings, design presentations, construction milestones | Developers, investors, design team | 3 weeks |
| **Investor Event** | Fund updates, market briefings, deal showcases | Investors, fund managers, principals | 4 weeks |
| **Vendor Showcase** | Product launches, material demonstrations, technology previews | Project teams, procurement, design | 2 weeks |
| **Community / Networking** | Industry mixers, panel discussions, educational sessions | Open or invite-only | 2 weeks |

## Procedure

1. **Receive event request.** Internal team or external partner submits an event request to the showroom manager with: event type, purpose, estimated attendance, date preferences, and any special requirements.
2. **Evaluate and approve.** Showroom manager reviews against zone availability (`sales-showroom/experience/ops-showroom-usage-and-zoning.md`), brand alignment, and capacity. SHB Group (Owner's Rep) approves investor events and events involving external speakers.
3. **Plan event.** Showroom manager creates an event plan: run of show, setup requirements, AV needs, catering, guest list, and assigned staff.
4. **Coordinate vendors (if applicable).** Apply vendor participation rules below.
5. **Execute event.** Showroom staff manages setup, guest reception, event flow, and teardown.
6. **Capture attendee data.** Collect attendee names, companies, and contact information (with consent) for CRM and follow-up.
7. **Post-event follow-up.** Within 48 hours: send thank-you communications, distribute any promised materials, and log business development leads.
8. **Post-event debrief.** Showroom manager files a brief event summary: attendance, feedback, leads generated, and lessons learned.

## Vendor Participation Rules

- **Invitation only.** Vendors participate by invitation from the showroom manager or Builiq Inc. (Procurement). No unsolicited vendor displays.
- **Brand consistency.** All vendor materials, signage, and displays must be pre-approved by SHB Studio (Design).
- **No direct sales on premises.** Vendors may demonstrate products and collect leads but may not process transactions at events.
- **Setup and teardown windows.** Vendors must arrive during the designated setup window and clear all materials within 2 hours of event conclusion.
- **Insurance.** Vendors providing physical demonstrations must carry liability insurance and provide a certificate of insurance 5 business days before the event.
