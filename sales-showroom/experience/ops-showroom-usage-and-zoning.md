# Showroom Usage and Zoning

## Purpose

Define how Foundry Rooms spaces are zoned, scheduled, and maintained — ensuring material displays, lease rooms, and event zones operate without conflict and support the sales, leasing, and brand experience objectives.

## Scope

All Foundry Rooms locations. Applies to showroom managers, sales teams, event coordinators, and visiting developers/investors.

## Zone Definitions

| Zone | Purpose | Access | Scheduling |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Material Display** | Curated display of finishes, fixtures, and construction materials from active and upcoming projects. | Open during business hours; guided tours by appointment. | Managed by showroom manager. Updated per project phase or product cycle. |
| **Lease Rooms** | Private rooms for developer meetings, investor presentations, lease signings, and design reviews. | By reservation only. | Booked through showroom manager; minimum 24-hour advance notice. |
| **Event Zones** | Flexible spaces for developer events, investor mixers, vendor showcases, and community programming. | By reservation only; public or invite-only per event type. | Booked through showroom manager; minimum 2-week advance notice for events. |
| **Back of House** | Storage, prep, and operations. | Staff only. | N/A |

## Procedure

### Material Display Management

1. **Curate display schedule.** Showroom manager coordinates with SHB Studio (Design) and Builiq Inc. (Procurement) to maintain a current material palette reflecting active projects.
2. **Rotate materials.** Update displays at each project phase milestone or when new products are sourced. Retired materials are returned to vendors or archived.
3. **Maintain display standards.** All materials labeled with: product name, manufacturer, spec section, and project reference. No unlabeled materials on display.

### Lease Room Scheduling

4. **Receive reservation request.** Internal teams or developers request a lease room through the showroom manager.
5. **Confirm availability and purpose.** Showroom manager confirms the room, time, and required setup (AV, catering, materials).
6. **Prepare the room.** Showroom staff sets up the room per the request. Confirm project-specific materials are staged if applicable.
7. **Post-use reset.** Room is reset to default configuration within 1 hour of use.

### Event Zone Scheduling

8. **Receive event request.** See `sales-showroom/experience/ops-events-and-networking.md` for the full event workflow.
9. **Confirm zone availability.** Showroom manager confirms no scheduling conflict with lease rooms or material display updates.
10. **Coordinate setup and teardown.** Showroom staff manages zone prep. External vendors follow the vendor participation rules in the events SOP.

## Controls

- **No conflicting bookings.** Lease rooms and event zones cannot be double-booked. The showroom manager is the single authority for scheduling.
- **No unapproved signage or branding.** All signage in display and event zones must be approved by SHB Studio (Design).
- **Material display integrity.** No materials may be removed from displays without showroom manager approval.
