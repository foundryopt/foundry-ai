# Leasing and Upgrade Feedback Loop

## Purpose

Capture buyer and tenant feedback during leasing, showroom interactions, and early occupancy — then route actionable insights back to Development and Design to improve future projects and inform upgrade/customization offerings.

## Scope

All Foundry Rooms locations and leasing activity across all entities. Applies to showroom managers, sales/leasing agents, the Development Concierge, and design consultants.

## Procedure

### Capture Feedback

1. **During showroom tours.** Sales agents record buyer/tenant reactions to materials, layouts, finishes, and pricing using a structured feedback form (see categories below).
2. **During lease negotiations.** Leasing agents document requested upgrades, customization asks, and objections.
3. **During early occupancy.** Property management collects move-in feedback within the first 30 days (see `property-management/ops-property-management-onboarding.md`).
4. **During events.** Showroom manager captures informal feedback during developer and investor events.

### Log and Classify

5. **Log feedback.** All feedback is recorded with: date, source (buyer/tenant/visitor), project, category, and verbatim comment or summary.
6. **Classify by category.** Assign one or more categories per the table below.
7. **Flag high-signal items.** Items that appear in 3+ independent feedback entries or represent a potential revenue opportunity are flagged as "high signal."

### Route to Functional Teams

8. **Route design feedback.** Items in the design, materials, or layout categories are routed to SHB Studio (Design) monthly.
9. **Route development feedback.** Items in the unit mix, pricing, or amenity categories are routed to the Development Concierge monthly.
10. **Route upgrade opportunities.** Items that represent a sellable upgrade or customization are routed to both Design and Development with a revenue estimate.
11. **Close the loop.** Functional teams acknowledge receipt and report back on whether insights were incorporated. Concierge updates the feedback log with the outcome.

## Feedback Categories

| Category | Examples | Routed To |
|---|---|---|
| **Design / Aesthetics** | "Love the kitchen tile but not the bathroom" | SHB Studio (Design) |
| **Materials / Finishes** | "Want hardwood instead of LVP" | SHB Studio + Builiq Inc. (Procurement) |
| **Layout / Floor Plan** | "Second bedroom too small for office use" | Development Concierge + SHB Studio |
| **Unit Mix** | "Need more 1BR units" / "Studios not selling" | Development Concierge |
| **Pricing / Value** | "Price per SF too high for this finish level" | Development Concierge + SHB Group |
| **Amenities** | "Expected a package room" / "Gym is too small" | Development Concierge |
| **Upgrade / Customization** | "Would pay extra for built-in closet system" | Development + Design |
| **Building Systems** | "Concerned about HVAC noise" | SHB Inc. (GC/CM) + Property Mgmt |

## Controls

- **No feedback older than 30 days unrouted.** All logged feedback must be classified and routed within 30 calendar days.
- **Monthly summary required.** Showroom manager compiles a monthly feedback summary for the Development Concierge and SHB Group (Owner's Rep).
- **Privacy.** Feedback is anonymized before routing. Personal information is stored separately and only used for direct follow-up with consent.
