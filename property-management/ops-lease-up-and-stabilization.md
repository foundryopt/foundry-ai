# Lease-Up and Stabilization

## Purpose

Define the process and metrics for taking a newly delivered property from initial leasing through stabilized occupancy — ensuring leasing velocity is tracked, underperformance is escalated, and the feedback loop to Development and Design is active.

## Scope

All newly delivered rental or mixed-use projects. Applies to property management teams, sales/leasing agents, the Development Concierge, and SHB Group (Owner's Rep).

## Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Lease-up** | The period from first unit availability to stabilization. |
| **Stabilization** | The project-defined occupancy threshold (typically 90–95% occupied) sustained for a defined period (typically 3 consecutive months). |
| **Absorption rate** | Units leased per month during lease-up. |
| **Concession** | Any incentive offered to tenants (free rent, reduced deposit, etc.) beyond standard lease terms. |

## Procedure

1. **Set targets at delivery.** Before first availability, SHB Group (Owner's Rep) and the Development Concierge set:
   - Target stabilization date.
   - Target absorption rate (units/month).
   - Concession authority and limits.
   - Reporting cadence (weekly during active lease-up, monthly after 75% occupied).
2. **Begin leasing.** Sales/leasing agents execute the leasing plan. Feed showroom feedback back per `sales-showroom/experience/ops-leasing-and-upgrade-feedback-loop.md`.
3. **Track weekly metrics.** During active lease-up, property management reports weekly:
   - Total units available, leased, occupied, and pending.
   - Absorption rate (trailing 4 weeks).
   - Average days on market per unit type.
   - Concessions granted (count and value).
   - Traffic and tour conversion rate.
4. **Review against targets.** Concierge and Owner's Rep review metrics weekly against the absorption target.
5. **Escalate underperformance.** Apply the escalation rules below if metrics deviate from targets.
6. **Adjust strategy.** Based on feedback and metrics, adjust pricing, concessions, marketing, or unit configuration. All pricing changes require Owner's Rep approval.
7. **Declare stabilization.** When the occupancy threshold is met for the defined period, the Concierge formally declares stabilization and transitions reporting to monthly.
8. **Post-stabilization review.** Within 30 days of stabilization, conduct a lessons-learned review with Development, Design, and Sales. Document insights for future projects.

## Stabilization Metrics

| Metric | Target | Reporting Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Occupancy rate | Project-defined (typically 90–95%) | Weekly during lease-up |
| Absorption rate | Project-defined (units/month) | Weekly during lease-up |
| Average days on market | < 45 days (standard market) | Weekly during lease-up |
| Tour-to-lease conversion | > 25% | Weekly during lease-up |
| Concession rate | < 5% of gross rent (guideline) | Weekly during lease-up |
| Tenant retention (post-stabilization) | > 70% renewal rate | Quarterly |

## Escalation Rules

| Condition | Action | Authority |
|---|---|---|
| Absorption rate < 75% of target for 2 consecutive weeks | Leasing team reviews strategy with Concierge | Development Concierge |
| Absorption rate < 50% of target for 4 consecutive weeks | Owner's Rep reviews pricing and concession strategy | SHB Group (Owner's Rep) |
| Stabilization date projected to slip > 30 days | Principal briefed; evaluate repositioning options | SHB Group (Principal) |
| Concession authority limit reached | Owner's Rep approves extended concessions or adjusts authority | SHB Group (Owner's Rep) |

## RACI

| Activity | Property Mgmt | Sales/Leasing | Development Concierge | SHB Group (Owner's Rep) | SHB Group (Principal) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Set targets at delivery | C | C | **R** | **A** | I |
| Execute leasing plan | C | **R** | I | I | — |
| Track weekly metrics | **R** | C | I | I | — |
| Review against targets | I | C | **R** | **A** | — |
| Escalate underperformance | I | I | **R** | **A** | I (if slip > 30 days) |
| Adjust pricing / concessions | I | C | **R** | **A** | — |
| Declare stabilization | I | I | **R** | **A** | I |
| Post-stabilization review | C | C | **R** | **A** | I |

**R** = Responsible, **A** = Accountable, **C** = Consulted, **I** = Informed
