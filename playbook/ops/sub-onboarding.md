# Subcontractor Onboarding

## Required Before First Day on Site

### Documents
- [ ] W-9 on file
- [ ] Certificate of Insurance (GL min $1M / $2M, WC as required)
- [ ] SHB listed as Additional Insured on GL policy
- [ ] Signed subcontract with scope of work
- [ ] Lien waiver template acknowledged

### Vetting
- [ ] References checked (min 2 past projects)
- [ ] License verification (where applicable — plumbing, electrical, roofing)
- [ ] OSHA compliance history (for trades with high incident rates)

### Onboarding
- [ ] Pre-con meeting attended
- [ ] Site safety orientation completed
- [ ] Schedule and milestone dates confirmed
- [ ] Payment terms confirmed (net 30 standard, draw-aligned for large scopes)
- [ ] Point of contact and emergency contact on file
