# Pre-Construction Checklist

## Before Breaking Ground

### Permits & Legal
- [ ] Building permit in hand (self-cert or standard)
- [ ] Demolition permit (if applicable)
- [ ] CDOT sidewalk/curb cut permits
- [ ] Utility disconnection notices filed
- [ ] Insurance certificates current (GL, WC, Builder's Risk)
- [ ] Sworn statement prepared

### Budget
- [ ] GMP locked and approved by Kuan
- [ ] Pro forma updated to CD-level costs
- [ ] Draw schedule aligned with lender requirements
- [ ] Contingency set (min 5% hard costs, 10% for rezone projects)

### Subcontractors
- [ ] All major trades under contract (demo, concrete, framing, MEP, drywall, finishes)
- [ ] Sub insurance certs collected and verified
- [ ] Lien waivers template distributed
- [ ] Pre-con meeting held with all trades

### Site
- [ ] Survey confirmed / updated
- [ ] Geotech report reviewed
- [ ] Utility locates called (JULIE)
- [ ] Temporary facilities plan (dumpster, porta-john, fencing)
- [ ] Neighbor notification (courtesy — not required but good practice)

### Schedule
- [ ] Master schedule in Smartsheet (Gantt)
- [ ] Takt schedule for production phases
- [ ] Procurement long-lead items identified and ordered
- [ ] Weather contingency buffer built in
