# Operations Guardrails

## Insurance

### Check sub insurance BEFORE they start — every project
**The rule:** Insurance certs expire. A cert from the last project doesn't cover this one. 
Verify GL, WC, and Additional Insured status at the start of every project, for every sub. 
One uninsured accident can bankrupt a project.

## Lien Waivers

### No payment without a lien waiver
**The rule:** Collect signed lien waivers with every pay app. Unconditional for prior payments, 
conditional for current. No exceptions. A missed lien waiver is a future lien on the property.

## Compliance

### OSHA visits are random — always be ready
**The rule:** Maintain site safety compliance every day, not just for inspections. 
Fall protection, hard hats, housekeeping. A single OSHA citation can shut down a site 
and the fine is the cheap part — the schedule hit is the real cost.

### Document everything you can't undo
**The rule:** Photograph and log every concealed condition — underground, in-wall, above-ceiling. 
Once it's covered up, the documentation is all you have. 
This protects you in warranty claims, insurance disputes, and code audits.
