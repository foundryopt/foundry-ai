# People Guardrails

## Hiring

### Hire for reliability, train for skill
**The rule:** In construction, someone who shows up every day, communicates proactively, 
and does clean work at 80% skill level is worth more than a genius who's unpredictable. 
You can teach technique. You can't teach showing up.

### References from other GCs, not just the sub themselves
**The rule:** Every sub will give you their best reference. Ask other GCs in Chicago 
who they use and who they've fired. The network knows who the problems are.

## Sub Red Flags

Watch for these — any two together means walk away:
- Significantly lowest bid (>15% below others)
- Can't provide insurance cert within 48 hours
- Pressures you to start before contract is signed
- Blames every past problem on the previous GC
- Won't commit to a specific schedule
- Cash flow complaints before work starts

## Investor Red Flags

Watch for these in LP conversations:
- Asks for guaranteed returns (doesn't understand real estate risk)
- Wants weekly project updates (will become a management burden)
- Can't articulate their source of funds (compliance risk)
- Delays wire after verbal commit more than twice
- Wants special terms not in the PPM
