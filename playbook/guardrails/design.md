# Design Guardrails

## Self-Certification

### The license is worth more than any project
**The rule:** Never rush a self-cert stamp. If there's any doubt about code compliance, 
take the extra day. One audit failure can put the license at risk. 
No single project's timeline is worth that.

### Coordinate before you stamp
**The rule:** Structural, MEP, and energy compliance must be fully coordinated 
before self-cert submission. "We'll figure it out in the field" is not an option 
when your name is on the stamp without a city reviewer backstop.

### Don't design what you can't build (with your subs)
**The rule:** Every design detail must be buildable by the subs you actually use. 
Exotic details that require specialty labor blow up budgets and schedules. 
If your framing crew can't execute it, redesign it.
